About Me

Born in Italy and raised in China. I moved to New York in 2011 and have been studying and living here ever since. In 2018, I graduated from Fordham University with a Bachelor of Science degree in Marketing and Fashion Studies as a minor. I just finished an immersive UX Design Program at Flatiron School and would like to work with a team that is creating user-centric projects.

While I was in Flatiron School, I worked through all phases of the UX process, from exploratory research, conducting Subject Matter Experts and user interviews, ideation and evaluation workshops, information architecture to wireframing and prototyping. I found myself fascinated by gaining insights from research, brainstorming new ideas with the team, and listening to user’s feedback. When I heared users saying, “Sorry, I was jumping into it, it’s easy to use”, that’s the moment I realized that I want to be a creative UX designer and design effortless user experiences.
